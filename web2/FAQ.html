FAQ

  • > What is a game buddy?

    A game buddy is someone that can play video games with you.

  • > How can I order a buddy?

    You can press the order buttom below each gamerbuddy's description.

  • > Can you fix xxx bugs?

    If the bug mentioned is a very serious bug and needs to be addressed immediately, do let us know through the live chat.

  • > I haven't received my invoice/receipt!

    The invoice should be in your email, you can also check your email's spam folder.

  • > Can I change the promised time after ordering a buddy?

    You may have a chat with the game buddy you ordered to have the reservation time changed, GamerHub holds no responsibilty for any losses for such changes.

  • > What is the main purpose of GamerHub?

    GamerHub's main purpose is to help users to find other players ( game buddy ) to play with easily.

  • > Can I make an order without creating an account?

    Unfortunately an account is mandatory for ordering buddies.

  • > How often does Leaderboard refreshes?

    It refreshes every minute to ensure reliability and for members to find good buddies.

  • > What is the purpose of the favorite section?

    It keeps a list of favorite buddies so they can be refered back with ease for members.

  • > I want to order a game buddy, I don't have a GamerHub account but I do have a Google account

    You can create a GamerHub account by using your Google account.

  • > Can I use symbols in my username?

    You are free to use any characters but avoid any words that may contains any vulgar words or sensitiive terms.

  • > I want to change my username

    You can have a chat with livechat to assist you with your request.

  • > Where is my favorite section? I can't find it!

    You can access it through user section upper right of the site once you have logged in into your account.
